Introduction (1/3): Roles


There are two types of participants in this activity: proposers and receivers. You'll be taking on the role of receiver.

The proposers have already done their part. Earlier today, a number of them were hired via Prolific. Their job was to offer a portion of some money to a receiver. Click Next to learn how they got that money.


Introduction (2/3): The Draw


Look at the picture of the box. There are {{Constants.total_balls}} balls in it. Of these, {{Constants.no_task_balls}} are No-task balls and one is a Task ball.




When the proposer joined the activity, the computer randomly drew a ball from the box. If the computer drew one of the Task balls, the proposer had to do a maths task. If they passed, they earned {{Constants.money}}. If the computer drew one of the No-task balls, the proposer skipped the task and was simply gifted {{Constants.money}}.

Since there are two Task balls, there was a two in {{Constants.total_balls}} chance the proposer would have to do the task.

Click Next to find out about the maths task.

Your role


Now that you've learned about the proposers, it's time to begin your part of the activity. Your part consists of three short stages:

  • In the first stage you'll learn a bit more about the proposer's offer.
  • In the second stage you'll be matched with a proposer and shown their offer.
  • In the third stage you'll be asked to guess whether the proposer you were matched did the maths task.
